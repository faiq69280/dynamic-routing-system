
Instructions : 

1. Run main.py,a grid window will be open 
2. Maximise the window for better experience 
3. Click on one of the cells of the grid to initialise start 
4. After 3, click on another one of the cells to initialise goal 
5. After 4, you are allowed to place obstacles 
6. Press enter to start animation of the agent tracing a path and making its way towards goal 

Note : You are allowed to place obstacles before or during the animation to observe changes in routes the agent will
take according to hurdles placed. In case no path exists, no path will be traced. You can remove one or two obstacles, press enter 
to start the animation again.
